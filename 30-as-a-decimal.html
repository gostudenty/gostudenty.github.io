Answer:

5 hundredths (5 x 0.01)

Step-by-step explanation:

The number word for 0.65 is sixty-five hundredths and is the same as its place value. That is because the number 65 is to the right of the decimal point, indicating the decimal in the hundredths place.

Reading Decimals

0.65Sixty-five hundredths

0.97Ninety-seven hundredths

Since the whole number has no value, we exclude that and the decimal point in the reading of decimal. We continue to the decimal numbers such as 65. We read it as usual (sixty-five) and since there are two digits, we know that it reaches to the hundredths place, therefore we read it as sixty-five hundredths.
