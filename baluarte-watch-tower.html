1. Ano Ang historya ng Baluarte Watch Tower? Pakibilis po kailangan ko ito bukas


Answer:

Ang Baluarte Watch Tower ay isang historical landmark sa Intramuros, isang distrito ng Maynila sa Pilipinas. Ang watch tower ay itinayo noong panahon ng pananakop ng mga Espanyol sa Pilipinas, at ginamit ito upang depensahan ang Intramuros mula sa mga mananakop na Kastila. Sa kasalukuyan, ang Baluarte Watch Tower ay isa sa mga pinakalumang mga bahagi ng Intramuros na nakaligtas sa pagka-destroy ng mga digmaan at sunog, at ito ay naging isang popular na tourist destination sa lungsod.

13. Jack and Roberto were assigned to guard a tower. Each was to watch for 5 hours, then rest 5 hours while the other watched. If Roberto began his first watch at 6:00 p.m., at what time will he begin his third watch?


6 + 5 = 11 pm (rest)

11 + 5 = 16 (4am; 2nd watch)

16 + 5 = 21 (9am; rest)

21 + 5 = 26 (2pm of the next day will be his 3rd watch)

6+5=11pm(end of the 1st watch), then he will rest for 5 hours so 11+5=16(4am,end of rest), after that he will begin his 2nd watch so 4am+5h=9am(end of 2nd watch), then rest for 5 hours again so 9am+5h=14 or 2pm, then his 3rd watch is 2pm.

23. BALUARTE: The Historical Watchtower of Luna, La Union 1. { Luna, La Union​


Answer:

Baluarte in Luna, La Union

Baluarte is a a 400-year-old watchtower located in the town of Luna, La Union. It is 5.6 meters high, and it faces the West Philippine Sea (South China Sea). The tower is made up of reddish bricks, and according to historians, the Spaniards built the tower so they can watch the seas. Pirate attacks are common during the Spanish period, and coastal towns take the brunt of the attacks from the Japanese, Chinese, and Moro pirates. When the pirates attack, the guards at the watch tower will immediately warn the citizens of Luna, and they will start to defend their town. During the Second World War, Baluarte served as a communication tower. The tower disintegrated to its present state because of erosion and weathering.
