12. The length of a rectangle is 3inches more than its width.Its area is 130inches2.Find the widtj and length of the rectangle​


Answer:

Width = 10 inches

Length = 13 inches

Step-by-step explanation:

Let, width = x

length = x + 3

area = 130 in²

Translate,

Area = Length x Width

130 = x(x + 3)

Use FOIL,

130 = x² + 3x

Rearrange

x² + 3x - 130 = 0

Factor,

(x + 13) (x - 10) = 0

Equate factors to zero,

x + 13 = 0

x1 = -13

x - 10 = 0

x2 = 10

Since we are dealing with measurements we disregard the negative root

Substitute the positive root,

width = 10 inches

length = 10 + 3 = 13 inches

16. A trapezoid has bases b1=9 inches and b2= 3inches and height h = 4inches.


AREA OF TRAPEZOID

[tex]\normalsize{\blue{\overline{\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad}}}[/tex]

A trapezoid has bases b₁=9 inches and b₂= 3 inches and height h = 4 inches.

[tex]\large\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION}:}[/tex]

The area of a trapezoid can be found using the formula

[tex]\boxed{A = \dfrac{1}{2} (b_1 + b_2) h}[/tex]

Where:

[tex]{b_1 \rm{(The \: 1st \: base \: of \: the \: trapezoid/ The \; wider\: base) = 9 \: inches}}[/tex][tex]{b_2 \rm{(The \: 2nd \: base \: of \: the \: trapezoid/ The \; smaller\: base) = 3\: inches}}[/tex][tex]{h \rm{(The \: height\: of \: the \: trapezoid) = 4 \: inches}}[/tex]

Substitute the given values into the formula, then solve for area:

[tex]A = \dfrac{1}{2} (b_1 + b_2) h[/tex]

[tex]A = \dfrac{1}{2} (9 + 3) (4)[/tex]

[tex]A = \dfrac{1}{2} (12) (4)[/tex]

[tex]A = 6 (4)[/tex]

[tex]A = \green{24}[/tex]

Hence, the area of the trapezoid is 24 inches.

28. The perimeter of a rectangle is 24inches. Find the dimensions if length is 3inches longer than its width


Answer:

The measure of the length is 7.5 inches and the width is 4.5 inches

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula for the perimeter of a rectangle is 2L + 2W. We know that the the perimeter is 24 inches. So our eqn. would look like this,

24 = 2L + 2W or

24 = 2 (L + W)

Since L is 3 inches longer than the width, we express L in terms of W.

24 = 2 ( W + 3 + W )

12 = 2W + 3 ;  divide both sides by 2

9 = 2W; subtract both sides with 3

4.5 = W'; divide both sides by 2

So our width is 4.5 inches which means our length is 7.5 inches
