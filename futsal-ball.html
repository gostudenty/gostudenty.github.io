1. what is the size of the ball for futsal​


Answer:

A FIFA approved Futsal ball must have a circumference between 24.61" and 25.0" - this is smaller than a traditional size 4 ball and bigger than a size 3 ball. Futsal balls are not sized the same way traditional soccer balls are sized (size 3, 4 & 5). Select futsal balls are offered in "Senior" for official size balls for adults and "Junior" smaller balls for youth players. Baden futsal balls are sized as size 4 for "official size" balls and size 3 for smaller "junior size" balls.

in futsal on the of sending the ball to a teammate is called

Answer:

Ball Pass

The ball Pass on Futsal is the act of sending the ball to a teammate. The Pass can be performed with any part of the body eligible by the Futsal Rules, such as the feet, thigh, chest, head, heel. The most used passing skill is performed by touching the ball with the inside of the foot, the so-called pass lead.

16. discuss the important events happened in the history of futsal and explain briefly the nature on how to play the game futsal​


Answer:

The origin of Futsal (Five-a-Side Soccer) can be traced back to Montevideo, Uruguay, in 1930 when Juan Carlos Ceriani devised a five-a-side version of soccer for youth competion in YMCAs. The game is played on basketball-sized courts, both indoors and out without the use of sidewalls.
