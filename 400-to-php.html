5. 1.In City Mall, a Php 400 shirt is marked, "Save 25%". What is the discounta. Php 250b.Php 200c. Php 150d.Php 1002. At 7% interest per year, what is the amount of interest on a loan of Php 80 000 for 6 months? a. Php 1 400 b. Php 2 800 c. Php 5 600 d. Php 1800​


Interest and Discount

Answer:

1. d. Php 100

2. b. Php 2 800

Step-by-step explanation:

In solving for discount, we multiply the rate by the original price. The rate is usually given as a percent.

Example: In City Mall, a Php 400 shirt is marked, "Save 25%". What is the discount?

    Given:

      Rate = 25%

      Original Price = Php 400

    Solution:

        Discount = Rate x Original Price

                         = (0.25) (400)

                         = 100

Thus, the discount is Php 100.

On the other hand, to solve for the interest we use the formula, I = Prt, where I is the interest, P is the principal amount, r is the rate, and t is the time.

Example: At 7% interest per year, what is the amount of interest on a loan of Php 80 000 for 6 months?

   Given

       Rate (R) = 7%

       Principal (P) = Php 80, 000

       time (t) = 6 months = 0.5 yr

    Solution:

       I = Prt

         = (80 000) (0.07) (0.5)

         = 2 800

Thus the amount of interest is Php 2 800.
