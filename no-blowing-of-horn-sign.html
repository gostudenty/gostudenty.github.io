2. what is the meaning No Blowing of horns​


As it says on the tin, the No Blowing of Horn sign is regulatory signage that orders a driver entering a particular area to avoid using his/her car horn. For the benefit of those who don't know, blowing your car horn is meant to warn other drivers on the road.
