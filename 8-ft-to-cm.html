feet <= feet + (inch / 12)

To convert cm to feet and inches, first divide the cm value by 30.48 to convert into feet. The integer part of the result is the foot value. To find the inch value, multiply the fractional part by 12.

cm to feet and inches formula

feet (x.y) = cm / 30.48

feet = x

inch = y * 12

Centimeter (centimetre) is a metric system length unit. 1 cm = 0.0328084 ft. The symbol is "cm".

Foot and inch are Imperial and United States Customary length units. 1 ft = 30.48 cm. The symbols are "ft" and "in".
