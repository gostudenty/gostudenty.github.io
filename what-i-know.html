2.Linear text refers to traditional text that needs to be read from beginning to the end while nonlinear text refers to text that does not need to be read from beginning to the end. As their names imply, linear texts are linear and sequential while non-linear and non-sequential.

3.The key difference between linear and nonlinear text is their reading path. In a linear text, a reader can make sense of the text by reading sequentially, from beginning to the end.
