11. A box has the following dimensions: 24 inches x 15 cm x 500 mm. Calculate the volume ofthe box in cubic meters​


Answer:

V = 0.046 m³

Explanation:

Convert in, cm, and mm to m.

l = 24 in × (2.54 cm / 1 in) × (1 m / 100 cm) = 0.61 m

w = 15 cm × (1 m / 100 cm) = 0.15 m

h = 500 mm × (1 m / 1000 mm) = 0.50 m

Solve for the volume.

V = lwh

V = (0.61 m)(0.15 m)(0.50 m)

V = 0.046 m³
