16. If the length of the diameter if a circle is 20 cm., what is the length of its radius? A.10 cmB. 20 cmс.5 cm D. 15 cm​


Answer:

A. 10cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Definition of Terms :

Diameter - is a chord that passes through the center of the circle.Radius - is a line segment that has one endpoint at the center and the other from the circle. The diameter is twice the length of the radius.

Solution :

Since D = 2r we'll just solve for r.

D = 2r

20 = 2r

20/2 = 2r/2

r = 10

Thus, the length of the radius is 10cm.
