12. what is motor and loco-motor?​


Answer:

Locomotor Skills are when we move our bodies from one location to another. They are a gross motor skill and the word locomotor comes from the noun locomotion which basically means the act of moving from place to place.

Answer:

A motor is a device that converts one form of energy into another, typically mechanical energy, in order to perform a specific task. Motors are commonly used in a variety of applications, such as powering machinery, vehicles, and electrical devices.

Locomotor refers to the ability to move or be moved from one place to another. In the context of human anatomy, locomotor skills refer to the ability to use the muscles and joints to move the body through space, such as walking, running, jumping, and climbing. In animals, locomotor abilities may include running, flying, swimming, or crawling.
