24. How many hours will it take to travel from bicol to manila if the car rate is 60km/hr. NOTE: Distance= (rate)(time)


DISTANCE, SPEED & TIME

Problem:

How many hours will it take to travel from Bicol to Manila if the car rate is 60 km/hr. The approximate distance from Bicol to Manila is 413.8 kilometers.

Answer: It will take approximately 6.90 hours to travel from Bicol to Manila at a speed of 60 kph.

Step-by-step explanation:

Recall that the formula for this kind of word problem is:

Distance = Rate x Time

By algebraic manipulation, we can solve for the time in hours. As we directly substitute the values where the distance given from Bicol going to Manila is 413.8 kilometers while the speed of the car is 60 kilometers per hour, we will be having:

413.8 = 60 x Time

Dividing both sides by 60 to isolate "Time" and get its value:

Time = 413.8/60Time = 6.90 hours

Therefore, when a car is constantly moving at a speed of 60 kph and the distance from Bicol to Manila is 413.8 kilometers, it will take 6.90 hours of travel.
