ΔU = Q - W

Q= 200 cal = 200 × 4.19 = 838 J

W = 1200J

ΔU = 838 - (-1200) = 2038 J

ΔU = 2038 J = (2038/4.19) cal = 486.39 cal ≈ 486 cal.

q=(2.0x10^1 g)(80 cal/g)

q=1,600 cal

30. Mhel loves cheese burgers. He enters a 24 hour cheese burger eating contest. During the contest Mhel eats a cheese burger every 15 minutes. How many cheese burgers will he eat during the entire contest? 32 cheese burgers 36 cheese burgers 54 cheese burgers 96 cheese burgers

24 hours(60mins/1hour) = 1,440 minutes

Mhel eaten cheese burger = 1,440/15
Mhel eaten cheese burger = 96 cheeseburgers
