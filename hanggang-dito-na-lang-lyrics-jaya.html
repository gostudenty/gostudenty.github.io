3. is jaya soprano or alto​


Is jaya soprano or alto

Jaya soprano is a soprano Voice. Here are the explanation.

Jaya is listed as a dramatic soprano, in particular. That makes it around low A (A3) to "big C" in a voice  range (C6). This voice typically has a lower tessitura and a deeper timbre than other sopranos. For further information: A soprano has the highest vocal range of all forms and is a type of classically female song voice. The style of soprano is typically split in soprano, soubrette, lyrical, spinto and dramatic.

The Philippine souls singer, rapper, record-maker, actress, dancer and host of mixed descent Jamaican  and Visayan, María Luisa, Ramsey Kagahastian-Gotidoc (* 21 March 1970), is formally named Jaya.  She was a GMA star but eventually moved to the ABS-CBN, where she is one of the lead juries for  the Philippine noontime variety show It's Showtime Tawag ng Tanghalan.
