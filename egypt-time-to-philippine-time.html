11. 1. The time in the Philippines is 6 hours ahead of Egypt. The flight Manila to Egypt takes 11 hours. If an airplane leaves Manila, Philippines by 6:00 a.m., at what time in Egypt will the plane arrive? Express Your Answer In 12-hour and 24-hour Clock Formats.PA-ANSWER PO NANG MAAYOS PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE​


To know what time in Egypt will the plane arrive, first, we will subtract 6 hours from 6:00 a.m. since the time in the Philippines is 6 hours ahead of Egypt. Then, add 11 hours to the difference.

06:00 – 6 hours = 00:00 --24 hour clock format

00:00 is same as 12:00 a.m.

00:00 + 11 hours = 11:00 a.m.

ANSWER:

12 hour clock format — 11:00 a.m.

24 hour clock format — 11:00 H

Therefore, the plane will arrive at 11:00 a.m. in Egypt.

What time is it in Warsaw, Poland, if it is 5:25 a.m. in the Philippines?4. Today is 1:00 a.m. Monday in New Delhi, India. During the same day, what time is it in thePhilippines?5. If it is 02:00 H in Cairo, Egypt, what time is it in the Philippines?​


1. 5 hours

2. 8:15 of Friday in the Philippines

3. 23:25 of yesterday in Warsaw, Poland

4. 2:30 of Monday in the Philippines

5. 8:00 of the same day in the Philippines

How?

1. 10:15 - 05:15 = 5 hours

2. Tokyo, Japan is an hour ahead of the Philippines, therefore decreasing an hour from 09:15 to find the time of the Philippines.

3. The Philippines is 6 hours ahead of Warsaw, therefore removing 6 hours from 5:25 to find its time. But that puts us to the previous day- I mean, night, so, while following the 24-hour format, the time is 23:25 of yesterday.

4. The Philippines is 2 and a half hours ahead of New Delhi, therefore adding the said time to 1:00 to find the Philippines' time.

5. The Philippines is 6 hours ahead of Cairo, therefore adding 6 hours to 02:00 to find the Philippines' time.
