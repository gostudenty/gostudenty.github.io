4. Ano ang pangunahing dayalekto ng Kalakhang Maynila? ​


Answer:

Ang Kalakhang Maynila (Ingles: Metropolitan Manila), tinatawag din bilang Pambansang Rehiyong Kapital[5] (Ingles: National Capital Region), ay ang kabiserang rehiyon at isa sa mga itinakdang kalakhang pook ng Pilipinas. Binubuo ito 16 na lungsod: ang Lungsod ng Maynila, Lungsod Quezon, Caloocan, Las Pinas, Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Marikina, Muntinlupa, Navotas, Parañaque, Pasay, Pasig, San Juan, Taguig, at Valenzuela, pati na rin ang bayan ng Pateros. Ang rehiyon ay may sukat na 619.57 square kilometre (239.22 mi kuw) at kabuuang populasyon na 12,877,253 noong 2015.

Taguig ([taˈɡiɡ]), officially the City of Taguig (Tagalog: Lungsod ng Taguig), is a 1st class highly urbanized city in Metro manila, Philipines.

According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 804, 915 people. [3] Located in the western shores of Laguna de Bay, the city is known for Bonifacio Global City, one of the leading financial centers of the Philippines.

Originally a fishing village during the Spanish and American colonial periods, it experienced rapid growth when former military reservations were converted by the Bases Conversion and Development Authority (BCDA) into mixed-use planned communities.
