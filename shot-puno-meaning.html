8. What is the meaning of 18 shots in a debut?


In the Philippines, when Filipino women reach their 18 years of age, it becomes one of their major celebration since it considered that they already reached a milestone in their lives. It also signifies that they are ready to step in into the adulthood world. These were based on the practice of the Filipino culture.  

Since they've already reach their 18 years of age, they were already called as "dalaga", which means a single young lady who has passed the stage of puberty. Some of the Filipino parents allowed their daughters to accept suitors as their child turns into eighteen. Also parents, will organize a huge celebration for their daughters called Debut.  

A traditional way to celebrate a debut is composed of a program consisting of 18 different things that give symbolize the celebration. These things may be flowers, wine, gifts, candles and others. Giving candles during the celebration means wishing and giving a brief yet sincere message for the debutant. 18 shots or simply giving a toast of wine which means a ritual which the wine or a drink is taken as an expression of honor or goodwill. This traditional way of celebrating a Debut may be formal but however, twists and gimmicks have been added nowadays.
