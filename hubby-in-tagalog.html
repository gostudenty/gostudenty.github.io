3. What Is Mean By Hubby


The term hubby was derived from the word husband. It refers to a married man. If you want to refer to your husband, informally, you may use hubby. In the modern times, some women use the term hubby to refer to a man whom they are in a relationship with, but whom they are not yet married with. These women feel that this man, or this hubby, has the characteristics of a would-be husband.

15. what is Hubbie's Law?(5) pa help Po!​


Hubble's law, also known as the Hubble–Lemaître law or Lemaître's law, is the observation in physical cosmology that galaxies are moving away from Earth at speeds proportional to their distance. In other words, the farther they are the faster they are moving away from Earth
