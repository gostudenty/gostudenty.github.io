4. cross word puzzle 15 across 15 down ​


Answer:

You can solve a 15 by 15 crossword puzzle by looking for common letters among the different clues. If there are a few common letters, then those common letters can be used to help fill in some of the empty squares. Once you have the common letters filled in, you can fill in the other empty squares based on the crossword clues.

After getting some clues filled in, it is often a good idea to look for patterns among the clues. If there are a few matching clues, then you can make an educated guess about the answer for the empty squares.
