19. the peri meter of a regular heptagon is 147 mm. what is the lenght of each side in cm?​


Step-by-step explanation:

The perimeter of a regular heptagon is the total length of all the sides of the heptagon. If the perimeter of a regular heptagon is 147 mm, and we want to find the length of each side in cm, we can use the formula for the perimeter of a regular heptagon to solve for the side length.

The formula for the perimeter of a regular heptagon is:

Perimeter = 7 * side length

In this case, we can rearrange the formula to solve for the side length:

Side length = Perimeter / 7

Plugging in the given values, we get:

Side length = 147 mm / 7 = 21 mm

To convert the side length from mm to cm, we can divide by 10:

Side length = 21 mm / 10 = 2.1 cm

Therefore, the length of each side in a regular heptagon with a perimeter of 147 mm is 2.1 cm.
