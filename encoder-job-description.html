2.73 hours


Step-by-step explanation:

Mary can do it in 6 hours

1 hour = 1/6 of the work


Rica can do it in 5 hours

1 hour = 1/5 of the work


Together,

1 hour = 1/6 + 1/5 = 11/30 of the work


Find the number of hours needed:

11/30 of the work = 1 hour

1/30 of the work = 1 ÷ 11 = 1/11 hour

30/30 of the work = 1/11 x 30 = 2.73 hours


Answer: They will take 2.73 hours
