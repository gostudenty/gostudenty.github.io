7. Mr lucido deposited 225 pesos in the bank. If he deposit consisted 29 bills, consisiting of 5 peso band 10 peso bills, how many 10 peso bills did he deposit?


Answer:

There are 16 pieces of 10-peso bills.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Total deposit: 225 pesosTotal bills: 29 piecesBill composition:  5-peso and 10-pesos

Let:

5-peso: x10-peso: y

Equation (1) where total bills is 29 pieces:

x + y = 29

y = 29-x

Equation (2) where total deposit amount is 225 pesos:

5x + 10y = 225

Solve for x, number of 5-peso bills by substitution 29-x to y in Equation (2):

5x + 10y = 225

5x + 10 (29-x) = 225

5x -10x + 290 = 225

-5x = 225 - 290

-5x/-5 = -65/-5

x = 13

There are 13 pieces of 5-peso bills.

Number of 10-pesos bills where x = 13:

y = 29-x

y = 29 - 13

y = 16

There are 16 pieces of 10-peso bills.

Check:

Equation (1): Total number of bills.

x + y = 29

13 + 16 = 29

29 = 29 (True)

Equation(2): Total amount of deposit.

5x + 10y = 225

5(13) + 10(16) = 225

65 + 160 = 225

225 = 225 (True)

27. Mr. Lucido deposited 225 pesos in the bank. if his deposit consisted of 29 bills, consisting of 5 peso and 10 peso bills, how many 10 peso bills did he deposit?


Answer:

y = 16 ten-peso bills

x = 13 five-peso bills

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the number of 5 peso bills

Let y be the number of 10 peso bills

He deposited 29 bills, hence, we got the equation:

x + y = 29 (Equation 1)

He deposited an amount of 225 pesos, hence, we got the equation:

5x + 10y = 225 (Equation 2)

You then get the value of either variables (we will use the value of x) in equation 1:

x + y = 29

x = 29 - y -----> this is the value of x

Now, you have the value of x, substitute this to equation 2:

5x + 10y = 225

5(29 - y) + 10y = 225

145 - 5y + 10y = 225

145 + 5y = 225

5y = 225 - 145

5y/5 = 80/5

y = 16 ten-peso bills

Use the value of y to get the value of x using equation 1:

x + y = 29

x + 16 = 29

x = 29 -16

x = 13 five-peso bills
