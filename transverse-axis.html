transverse axis = 4

foci (4, 0), (-4, 0)

Solution:

transverse axis = 2a

4 = 2a

a = 2

a² = 4

foci = (±c , 0)

c² = a² + b²

b² = c² - a²

b² = 4² - 2²

b² = 16 - 4

b² = 12

[tex]\[\begin{array}{l}\frac{{{x^2}}}{{{a^2}}} - \frac{{{y^2}}}{{{b^2}}} = 1\\\\\frac{{{x^2}}}{4} - \frac{{{y^2}}}{{12}} = 1\end{array}\][/tex]

or[tex]\[\frac{{3{x^2} - {y^2}}}{{12}} = 1\][/tex]

3x² - y² = 12
