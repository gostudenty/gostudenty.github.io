Answer:

How to convert inches to cm?

1 Inch (in) is equal to 2.54 cm. To convert inches to cm, multiply the inch value by 2.54.

For example, to find out how many cm is 12 inches, multiply 12 by 2.54, that makes 30.48 cm is 12 in.

inches to cm formula

cm = inch * 2.54

What is an Inch?

Inch is an imperial and United States Customary systems unit of length, equal to 1/12 of a foot. 1 in = 2.54 cm. The symbol is "in".

What is a Centimeter?

Centimeter (centimetre) is a metric system unit of length, equal to 1/100 of a meter (metre). 1 cm = 0.3937007874 in. The symbol is "cm".

4. what is The volume of 5inches length 5inches width, 5inches heigth​


PROBLEM:

What is the volume of 5 inches length, 5 inches width, 5 inches height?

SOLUTION:

The given measures tells us that the object has a shape of a cube since the measure of the height, width and the length are the same.

VOLUMEamount of space of a certain three-dimensional objectit has a standard unit of cubic meter (m³)

In finding the volume of a cube, just need to multiply the given measures of length, width and height to get the volume ( V = lwh ) or simply multiply the measure of the edge three times by itself ( V = e³ ).

[tex]\begin{array}{l}\rm V = e³ \\ \rm V =( 5 \: in)³ \\ \rm V = 5 \: in×5 \: in×5 \: in \\ \rm V = 25 \: in²× 5 \: in \\ \rm V = 125 \: in³\end{array}[/tex]

ANSWER:

The volume of the given object is 125 cubic inches.

Any triangle whose sides are in the ratio 3:4:5 is known as Pythagorean Triple. so this means if you multiply a constant number to the ratio, the resulting triangle or sides measurement will be congruent.
