5. aimee bought 9.8 meter of geena satin cloth.One-fifth of the cloth was damage by rats and the rest was used to make 7 similar blouse 1.how much cloth did she use for each blouse?2.if she sold each blouse for 178.25 how much money would she receive in all?​


1. Aimee used 1.12 m of cloth for each blouse. 2. She would receive 1,247.75.

Step-by-Step Explanation:

Word Problem

The problem above is asking for the following:

The length of cloth Aimee used for each blouse.The amount of money she would receive in all if each blouse is sold for 178.25.

Given:

the total length of the cloth - 9.8 m

the length of the cloth that was damaged - ⅕ of the cloth or ⅕ of 9.8 m

number of blouses - 7

cost of each blouse - 178.25

Solution:

1. To find the length of cloth that Aimee used for each blouse, you have to know first the length of the remaining cloth. Then, divide it by the number of blouses.

9.8 m - (⅕ × 9.8 m)

= 9.8 m - 1.96 m

= 7.84 m

The remaining cloth is 7.84 m.

7.84 m ÷ 7 = 1.12 m

Aimee used 1.12 m of cloth for each blouse.

2. To find the amount of money she would receive, multiply 7 by 178.25.

7 × 178.25 = 1,247.75

She would receive 1,247.75.

30. Geena obtained a loan of 48,000 from the rural cooperative at 9.5%interest rate.on the due date,she paid 72,000.when was the loan due?


Answer:

t = 5.2632 years

(about 5 years 3 months)

Step-by-step explanation:

Equation:

      t = I / Pr

First, converting R percent to r a decimal

            r = R/100 = 9.5%/100 = 0.095 per year,

Second, calculate for the interest amount

            = amount paid-loan amount  

           =72000-48000

           =24000

then, solving our equation

        t = I / Pr

         = 24000 / ( 48000 × 0.095 )

         =24000 / 4560

         = 5.26315789474

         = 5.2632 years
