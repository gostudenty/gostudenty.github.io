23. F= C × 9/5 + 321. 190 degrees Centigrade to degrees Fahrenheit. C= F - 32 × 5/92. 375 degrees Fahrenheit to degrees Centigrade. ​


FORMULA:

From °C to °F

C × 9/5 + 32 or C × 1.8 + 32

From °F to °C

F - 32 × 5/9 or F - 32 × 0.556

QUESTION WITH SOLUTION:1) 190 degrees Centigrade to degrees Fahrenheit.

Solution:

n °F = 190 × 1.8 + 32

n °F = 342  + 32

n °F = 374

Answer:

374 °F

2) 375 degrees Fahrenheit to degrees Centigrade.

Solution:

n °C = 375 - 32 × 0.556

n °C = 343 × 0.556

n °C = 190.708  or 190.7

Answer:

190.7 °C
