1. Examples of Tula about love​


"MAHAL NA KITA"

By: Frecy Empillo

'Di ko maipaliwanag ang aking nadarama

Tuwing kausap kita, iba ang hatid mong saya

Para mo akong dinadala sa ibang mundo kung saan tayo lang dalawa

Wala akong ibang makita kundi ikaw, wala ng iba.

Sa mga simpleng banat at biro mo

Pigil ang mga tili at tawa ko.

Sa mga mensahe mo mapaumaga hanggang gabi,

Ako'y parang kinikiliti.

Marinig ko lamang ang boses mo

Buo na ang araw ko

Para itong musika sa aking pandinig

Mas gugustuhin ko pang makausap kita kaysa sa musika ay makinig.

Sa bawat pagtatapos ng ating usapan

Para akong bumababa mula sa kalangitan.

Gayunpama'y mahimbing akong nakakatulog sapagkat puso't isip ko'y ikaw ang laman

Maging sa paggising ko kinaumagahan, naroon ka at hindi mo ako nilulubayan.

Kahit anong gawin ko

Palagi kang nagsusumiksik sa puso't-isip ko

Sa pagmulat ng mata

Mukha mo ang palaging nakikita.

Hindi ka maalis sa aking isipan

Sa puso ko ika'y nananahan

Puso ko'y ginawa mong tambayan

Hindi mo ito maiwan-iwan.

Kahit anong pagsusumiksik ng takot

Takot na baka ako'y muli na namang mapaikot

Ito'y hindi na alintana

Sapagkat mahal na kita, yun ang tanging mahalaga.

3. tula about first love​


You taught me how beautiful life is,
Everything from your words, your touch, your kiss.

You gave me hope, you gave me love,
And I know you were sent from heaven above.

The way you showed me the person I could be,
It's something you, and only you, would see.

You were always different and unique,
But I never guessed it was me you would seek.

How someone like me would end up with someone like you,
I honestly don't believe it; it cannot be true.

But it is, and I know every day
That I have YOU, and it's needless to say

You've made my life beautiful and taught me how to live.
To show you how much I'm grateful, anything I'd give.

So thank you for coming into my life.
In doing so you've taken away the sorrow and strife.

You put a smile on my face and a skip to my stride
And I know you'll always be there by my side. <3

4. Help me composed a Tula about idol and the girl who was his childhood friend and his childhood friend girl fell in love with the idol and the idol also fell in love with his friend.


...

Childhood friends they were,

Growing up in a small town

Playing games, laughing, and dreaming,

And watching stars as the sun goes down.

The boy was a dreamer,

An aspiring musician with a guitar,

While the girl was his closest confidant,

A friend with whom he shared every scar.

Years passed, and the boy found success,

Becoming an idol loved by many,

While the girl remained his constant supporter,

Cheering him on with love aplenty.

But one day, as fate would have it,

The idol saw the girl in a new light,

For her beauty and kindness had captured his heart,

And he knew he had to make things right.

At first, the boy was heartbroken,

Seeing his childhood friend with another,

But as time went on, he realized the truth,

That their love was unlike any other.

For the girl and the idol, it was destiny,

A love that bloomed from a lifelong bond,

And though the boy had lost his love,

He knew their friendship would forever carry on.

So he smiled and played his guitar,

Pouring his heart and soul into his art,

For he knew that the love he once had,

Was always there, deep in his heart.


5. Gumawa ng tula about love


Ikaw Lang Sapat Na

Sa aking paghahanap sa’yo ko nahagilap,

Ang pagsintang pangarap ay sa’yo ko nalasap,

Sa gitna ng kariktan ikaw ang pahingahan,

Sa iyong paghapuhap dama’y kaginhawaan.

Kahit may pasubali hindi ito hahadlang,

Upang maipaglaban pagsintang walang kaparang,

Ipaglalayo man hindi magiging dahilan,

Upang tagpisin ang ating pagmamahalan.

Pagbibiyakin ma’y muli kong bubuohin,

Sapagkat ikaw lang sapat na ang para sa akin,

At kahit maraming mata ang siyang huhusga,

Hindi ito magsisilbing salabid para sa’ting pagiging malaya.

Kahit mali man sa paningin nila,

Ang mahalaga wala tayong inaapakang iba,

Hindi man tama para sa kanila,

Ang mahalaga totoo tayo kaysa sa kanila.

7. pa help po paggawa po ng Tula about love​


KAHIT SAAN

Kung sa mga daang nilalakaran mo,

may puting bulaklak ang nagyukong damo

na nang dumaan ka ay biglang tumungo

tila nahihiyang tumunghay sa iyo. . .

Irog, iya’y ako!

Kung may isang ibong tuwing takipsilim,

nilalapitan ka at titingin-tingin,

kung sa iyong silid masok na magiliw

at ika’y awitan sa gabing malalim. . .

Ako iyan, Giliw!

Kung tumingala ka sa gabing payapa

at sa langit nama’y may ulilang tala

na sinasabugan ikaw sa bintana

ng kanyang malungkot na sinag ng luha

Iya’y ako, Mutya!

Kung ikaw’y magising sa dapit-umaga,

isang paruparo ang iyong nakita

na sa masetas mong didiligin sana

ang pakpak ay wasak at nanlalamig na. . .

Iya’y ako, Sinta!

Kung nagdarasal ka’t sa matang luhaan

ng Kristo’y may isang luhang nakasungaw,

kundi mo mapahid sa panghihinayang

at nalulungkot ka sa kapighatian. . .

Yao’y ako, Hirang!

Ngunit kung ibig mong makita pa ako,

akong totohanang nagmahal sa iyo;

hindi kalayuan, ikaw ay tumungo

sa lumang libinga’t doon, asahan mong. . .

magkikita tayo!
