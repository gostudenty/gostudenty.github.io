3. a triangle has a base of 60 cm and height of 16 in . find its area in square feet


First, let's convert the base and height into feet.

cm to ft: 1 foot = 30.48 cm

60 cm ÷ 30.48 cm = 1.968503937... ≈ 1.97 ft.

in to ft: 1 foot = 12 in

16 in ÷ 12 in = 1.33333... ≈ 1.33 ft

Now, let's solve for the area. The area of a triangle is (bh)/2, wherein b is the base and h is the height.

A = (bh)/2

A = [(1.97 ft)(1.33 ft)]2

A = 2.6201 ft²/2

A = 1.31005 ft²

A = 1.31 ft²

The area of the triangle is 1.31 ft²
