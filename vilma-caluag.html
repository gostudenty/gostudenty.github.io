Talambuhay ni Vilma Ambat

10. 30. Aling Vilma is twice as old as his son Joey. Express Joey's age in algebra equation if Aling Vilma's age is 60.​


Answer:

[tex]y = 2x[/tex]

Or

[tex]x = \frac{y}{2} [/tex]

Explanation:

Let x be the age of Joey and y for Aling Vilma. So,

[tex]y= 2x[/tex]

[tex]60 = 2x[/tex]

[tex]x = \frac{60}{2} [/tex]

[tex]x = 30[/tex]
