22. PROBLEM#1. JOHNNY COMBINED 7/4 CUPS OF PANCAKE MIX AND 3/4 CUP OF WATER TO MAKE PANCAKE BATTER. IF HE USES 1/4 OF A CUP OF BATTER TO MAKE EACH PANCAKE HOW MANY PANCAKES CAN JOHNNY MAKE FROM HIS BATTER


10 pancakes

Johnny can make 10 pancakes from his batter.

Step-by-step explanation:

Two-Step Word Problem

The problem is asking for the number of pancakes that Johnny can make from his batter. There are two operations involve in finding the answer to the problem. They are addition and division.

Find the total cups of batter that Johnny have by adding the cups of pancake mix and water.Find the number of pancakes that he can make by dividing the total cups of batter by a cup of batter for each pancake.

Given:

cups of pancake mix - ‌7⁄4‌

cup of water - ¾

Solution:

Step 1: Add

Since the fractions are similar, just add the numerators and copy the denominator.

‌7⁄4‌ + ¾ = ‌10⁄4‌

Step 2: Divide

To divide, change the sign to multiplication and reciprocate the second fraction.

10⁄4‌ ÷ 1⁄4‌

= 10⁄4‌ × 4⁄1

= 40⁄4‌ or 10

Final Answer:

10 pancakes

29. In cooking pancakes, 2 eggs are needed for every 1 cup of hot cake mix. How many cups of hotcakes mix if the baker uses 7 eggs.


We can use ratio and proportion here. We can express the sentence "In cooking pancakes, 2 eggs are needed for every 1 cup of hot cake mix" as 2 : 1.
Now, let x represent the number of cups of hotcake mix if the baker uses 7 eggs.
2 : 1 = 7 : x
(2)(x) = (7)(1)
2x = 7
2x/2 = 7/2
x = 7/2
x = 3 and 1/2
Therefore, the cups of hotcake mix that is made if the baker uses 7 eggs is 3 and 1/2.
