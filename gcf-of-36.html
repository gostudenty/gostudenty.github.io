Answer : The GCF of 33 and 36 is 3. To calculate the greatest common factor of 33 and 36, we need to factor each number (factors of 33 = 1, 3, 11, 33; factors of 36 = 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 9, 12, 18, 36) and choose the greatest factor that exactly divides both 33 and 36, i.e., 3.
