7. 9. What type of kitchen knives that is used to prepare salad greens, veg a. Butcher knife b. Boning knife Roast beef slicer d. Fruit and sa re peel and section fruits and ve sort​


Answer:

Fruit and salad knife or lettuce  knife

Explanation:

This knife has a serrated blade that can facilitate the cutting of salad greens, vegetables, and even garnishes. This is usually made out of food safe plastic material such as nylon. The serrated edge of this knife made it easy for the knife to cut through the fibrous parts of fruits and vegetable.

The plastic blade of the knife makes the knife perfect for salad making, as plastic is a non-reactive material unlike the stainless or high-carbon steel in other kitchen knife. The plastic blade ensures that the edges of of the fruits and vegetables won't turn brown as what would happen if they're cut with ordinary kitchen knives.
