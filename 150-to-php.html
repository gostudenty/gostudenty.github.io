17. Nine people gave contributions in pesos: 100, 200, 100, 300, 300, 200, 200, 150, 100, and 100 for a door prize. What is the median contribution?a. Php 100 b. Php 150 c. Php 175  d. Php 200


Answer:

C. Php175

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the median of a set, simply arrange the numbers or elements in either ascending order or descending order then get the middle number. If there are two middle numbers, add them together and divide by two. The quotient is the median.

{100, 200, 100, 300, 300, 200, 200, 150, 100, 100}

{100, 100, 100, 100, 150, 200, 200, 200, 300, 300}

The two middle numbers are 150 and 200.

150 + 200 = 350

350/2 = 175

The median is 175.
