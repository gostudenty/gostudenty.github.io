• The type of interaction exist between the organisms is benefit and not affected.

Which organism benefited from the interaction?

• The organism that is benefited from the interaction is the Fern.

• COMMENSALISM - One organism benefits from the host organism, while the host organism is neither positively nor negatively affected.
