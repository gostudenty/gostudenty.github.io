3. find the base of a triangle if its height is 22cm and its area is 165cm (square)


A = 1/2 bh
2A = bh
2(165) = 22b
330 = 22b
b = 330/22
b = 15

The base of a triangle is 15 cm. 165= bh/2
165=b(22)
165/22=b
7.5=b 

area po siya ng triangle so 7.5x2= 15cm. 

its base is 15cm.
