7. Ano Ang pag kakaiba ng major chord at minor chord


Answer:

Aside from the theory, which we’ll get to in a moment, the main difference between major and minor chords is how they sound. Major chords have a brighter, happier sound while minor chords produce sounds that are sad and melancholy. Songwriters choose their chords carefully to evoke emotions in the listener and give musical form to their lyrics. As you become a more experienced player, you will likely be able to tell exactly which chords are being played within a song.

Now, about the theory: The difference between major and minor chords is this: A major chord consists of a 1st, 3rd, and 5th degree of a major scale while a minor chord is made from the 1st, flatted 3rd, and 5th degrees of a major scale. So the only difference is the 3rd degree. You can see this clearly when you compare certain chords such as an open A Major with an open A Minor chord. The only difference is the note on the second string; changing that one note profoundly affects the sound.

9. 1. Ano ang ibig sabihin ng linyang "... himig ng ilog nakinukubli ng mga punong-kahoy?”​


Ang ibig sabihin ng linyang ito ay marahil ang paghadlang ng mga bagay-bagay sa isa pang naiibang layunin na nais kumawala. Minsan ay gustohin man ng isang tao na magpahayag ng kanyang kaisipan, maraming pagsusulit at hamon ang pipigil dito. Maaari rin itong kumahulugan sa isang bagay na nagtatago sa loob ng di-umano'y mga dingding, nakikita man o hindi. Ibig sabihin ay mayroon tayong itinatago paminsan na ating ipinagdadamot sa iba. Minsan ay may ibang humahadlang sa atin para ilantad ito, ngunit paminsan ay tayo mismo ang punong-kahoy na kumukubli sa ating mga hangad.

Karagdagang Eksplanasyon:

ang iba pang kahulugan ng salitang 'kinukubli' ay paghadlang, pagtago, o pagharangdahilan sa salitang 'himig', ipinararating ng linya na may bagay na gustong kumawala; isang bagay na may kagandahan taglay ngunit itinatago ng nakapaligid na sagabal

Mga Keyword: talasalitaan, tayutay, talinghaga, tagalog, Filipino, kinukubli, kubli, himig, ilog, ibig, sabihin
