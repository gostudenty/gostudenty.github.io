8. It is between eight to 12 words


It is called The Fraction. Here we will show you how to write and say the fraction of between eight to 12 words. Typically, it is written with 8/12.

But there are actually a few ways of saying and writing 8/12.

- A fraction like 8/12 means 8 divided by 12, so you could say: eight divided by twelve.

- Another common way of writing and saying 8/12 is: eight over twelve.

- However, we think the best way of writing and saying the fraction 8/12 is:

eight twelfths.

THE FRACTION

A portion of the whole is referred to as a fraction. In the field of mathematics, the value of the number is written as a quotient, which is calculated by dividing the numerator by the denominator. Both of the numbers in a simple fraction are considered to be integers. A complex fraction either has a fraction in the numerator or the denominator as one of its components. The numerator of a correct fraction is always smaller than the denominator of the fraction.

There are always two components to a fraction: the top number and the bottom number. These two components are referred to as the numerator and the denominator in mathematical jargon. Don't put too much stock in your ability to recall all of their names.

The term "fractional equation" refers to an equation in which one or more of the terms is a fraction. In order to solve an equation containing fractions, you must first remove the fractions from the equation by multiplying both sides of the equation by the LCD (least common denominator) of each term.

26. 12. __________ are like word puzzle.


Correct Answer:

The answer for Question #12 is...

Crossword (Puzzle) are like word puzzle.

Explanation:

Crossword puzzle is the same as word puzzle because when you play the puzzle/game, you should find the words that are missing.

The differences between these is how you should find it. To find the word (Answer) in the crossword puzzle is to figure out the word with the given definition but to find the word (Answer) in the word puzzle, you should find it through a box full of words. The words (in the word puzzle) can be found horizontally, vertically, forward or backward depending on instructions.

Answer:

Explanation:

In linguistics, an affix is a morpheme that is attached to a word stem to form a new word or word form. Affixes may be derivational, like English -ness and pre-, or inflectional, like English plural -s and past tense -ed. They are bound morphemes by definition; prefixes and suffixes may be separable affixes.
