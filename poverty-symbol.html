Poverty is defined as a state or condition in which a person or community lacks the financial resources and necessities for a basic way of life. Poverty is defined as having an income level from employment that is so low that basic human needs cannot be met. Poverty-stricken individuals and families may lack adequate housing, clean water, nutritious food, and medical care. Each country may have its own criteria for determining the poverty line and calculating the number of people living in poverty.Poverty has a significant impact on children.
