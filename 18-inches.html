24. A parallelogram has a base of 7 inches and a height of 2 inches. What is the area of this parallelogram? Select one: a. 14 inches2 b. 18 inches c. 18 inches2


Answer:

a. 14 inches2

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]\sf Area \ of \ parallelogram = Base \times Height[/tex]

Given:

[tex]\sf Base = 7 \ inches[/tex][tex]\sf Height = 2 \ inches[/tex]

By substitution,

[tex]\sf Area \ of \ parallelogram = 7 \ inches \times 2 \ inches[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf Area \ of \ parallelogram = 14 \ inches^2[/tex]

Hence, we select Option a.
