13. Directions: Identify what organ systems are involved in the following activities.1. Swimming2. drawing3. computing math problems4. singing5. reciting a poem​


Answer:

Human Body Organ Systems

Here are the answers:

Swimming – respiratory, skeletal, and muscular system Drawing – sensory, skeletal, and muscular system Computing math problems - nervous system Singing - respiratory system and digestive system Reciting a poem - respiratory system and digestive system

Explanation:

When you are swimming, the muscles and the skeleton are working together to help you move across the water. The respiratory system is also used to help you breathe when you need to catch air. When you are drawing, you need your eyes (sensory system) and your arms (skeletal and muscular system). When you are computing math problems, you are using your brain, which is part of the nervous system. Singing and reciting poems would require power from your vocal chords, lungs, diaphragm, and the mouth (including the tongue, lips, and teeth).

23. The architect’s drawing of a rectangular swimming pool has dimensions of 5 cm by 7.5 cm on a blueprint. If the length of the actual pool is 21ft, what is the area of the actual swimming pool in square feet?


Answer:

294 ft²

Step-by-step explanation:

first find the value of its width

5/7.5 = x/21

cross multiply

5(21)=x(7.5)

105=7.5x

x=14ft

lets x=W

A=LxW

A=21ft x 14ft

A=294ft²
