2. what is the Definition of Kagul Muslim Mindanao Instrument??


A Kagul is a Philippine bamboo scraper gong/ slit drum of Maguindanaon people.
Kagul is also called tagutok, bantula, taguntong or karuntang. It is played with two beayers, other scarping the jagged edge and the one making a beat. It looks like a bamboo scrapper gongs or slit drum.
