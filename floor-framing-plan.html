2. How to do a Floor plan with roof framing plan and with front side​


Answer:

Creating a floor plan with roof framing plan and front side can be done using various tools and techniques. Here are the general steps you can follow:

Sketch the outline of the floor plan on paper, including the placement of walls, doors, and windows.Determine the location and spacing of load-bearing walls and columns, as well as the type of roofing system you will use.Draw the roof framing plan, including the placement of rafters, trusses, and any other structural components.Mark on the floor plan the location of any attic spaces or storage areas that will be created by the roof framing.Create a front side elevation view of the building, showing the height and placement of the roof, as well as any architectural features such as dormers or gables.Finally, use a software like AutoCAD, SketchUp, or Revit to create a more detailed and accurate floor plan, roof framing plan, and front elevation view.

It's important to note that creating a floor plan with roof framing plan and front side, specially with a software, might require some technical skills and knowledge in architecture and construction. If you're not familiar with this, you might want to consider hiring an architect or a professional to help you with the design process.

Answer:
