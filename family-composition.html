3. What is family composition?


Answer:

What is family composition?

A family is usually composed of parents (mother and father) and children. This type of family is called nuclear family. And it is the most common type of family.

But a family can also be composed of not only parents but also relatives, which is called extended family. And if a family has only 1 parent (either mother or father) who single-handedly raised the kids, it is called Single parent family.

But if only a man and woman living together without kids, it is also a family, which is called Childless family.
