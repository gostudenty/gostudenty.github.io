10. Christmas party opening remarks tagalog


Christmas Party Opening Remarks

Isang mapayapa at makabuluhang umaga sa ating lahat; ngayon ay ipinagdiriwang natin ang kaarawan ng ating mahal na Panginoong Hesus. Tayo ay magsaya at gawin nating kapakipakinabang ang araw na ito; tayo’y magpasalamat sapagkat sakabila ng lahat ay patuloy pa rin tayong iniingatan ng ating Panginoon. Bigyan natin ng ating matatamis na ngiti ang ating mga katabi at pasalamatan natin sila sapagkat nakasama natin sila sa mga taon na nakalipas. Lagi natin pakatandaan na ang atin Panginoon ay mapagpatawad at mapagpakumbaba kaya’t marapat lamang na siya ay ating tularan. Mahalin natin ang ating kapwa at mamuhay ng may dignidad.

Isang maligayang pasko sa ating lahat; hiling ko ang kaligayahan at kapayapaan sa panimula ng pagdiriwang na ito.

Christmas is an annual sacred Christian holiday that commemorates the birth of Jesus Christ, the spiritual leader and founder of Christianity. While many people celebrate Christmas to honor Jesus's birth, it's also celebrated as a cultural holiday across the globe.

Answer:The message of Christmas, as celebrated by Christians, is that God sent his son Jesus Christ to earth as a baby born to the Virgin Mary in order to save humanity from sin. The holiday commemorates the birth of Jesus and serves as a reminder of the love, grace, and forgiveness that he offers to all people. The message of Christmas is one of hope, joy, and renewal, as it celebrates the idea that through Jesus, people can have a fresh start and a new beginning. It is also a time to remember the importance of love, compassion, and generosity, and to reach out to others in a spirit of goodwill.

23. opening remarks for christmas party tagalog


Welcome words/Opening remarks

Kumusta, lahat, at maligayang pagdating sa aming Christmas party! Lubos kaming natutuwa na makakasama kayong lahat dito ngayon upang ipagdiwang ang pinakamagagandang oras ng taon. Sulitin natin ang espesyal na okasyong ito.

Welcome words are speeches spoken at celebrations, parties, and others. The meaning of the opening speech is a sentence that is delivered before the main event begins. Generally it contains a welcome from the presenter and is continued by important parties or figures who give advice or hope to the audience.

Purpose of ForewordMake the audience follow the directions from giving the speech (Usually done by the MC or host)Give an overview of the main events.Encouraging core activities to be carried out as well as possible.Motivate the audience to take an action.Making events less boring and more lively.

Types of GreetingsThe opening remarks are usually spoken by the master of ceremonies before inviting stakeholders to give official remarks.Briefing remarks, namely remarks that direct the audience to follow the event as well as possible. Usually read by the host.The inauguration speech, can be in the form of a spontaneous speech without text or using a speech text that has been prepared on a piece of paper.Accountability remarks, namely the opening speech that opens an accountability report on events or activities that have been or have been held.
