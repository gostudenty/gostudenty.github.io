7. 1. A kite is to be constructed using sticks and colored paper, How many square centimeters of colored paper will be used for a kite with the given dimensions?


Answer:

The square centimeters of colored paper to be used for a kite with the given dimensions would be 80 sq. cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the number of sq. cm of the colored paper to be used

x = 1/2 [(12 cm + 4 cm) (5 cm + 5 cm)]

x = 1/2 (16 cm) (10 cm)

x = 1/2 (160 sq. cm)

x = 80 sq. cm

17. What made the color of paper differ from its original color?​


ANSWER:

From brand to brand, papers have different chemical compositions, additives, textures, finishes, etc., all of which react differently with inks and toners of digital and conventional presses. All things being equal, the same design printed on two different white card stocks may vary slightly in color.
